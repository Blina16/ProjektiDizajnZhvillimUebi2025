University SAT Scores and Financial Aid Information

Harvard University

SAT Score Range: 1440 - 1570

Financial Aid: Need-based aid available, covering up to 100% of demonstrated need.

Harvard offers generous financial aid packages that include scholarships, grants, and work-study programs to ensure affordability for students from all backgrounds.

Stanford University

SAT Score Range: 1440 - 1570

Financial Aid: Need-blind admissions with aid covering tuition for families earning less than $150,000 annually.

Stanford provides need-based aid and scholarships, ensuring accessibility for students from diverse financial backgrounds.

Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

SAT Score Range: 1510 - 1580

Financial Aid: Need-based aid offered, covering full tuition for families earning under $90,000.

MIT ensures that admitted students can afford their education through scholarships, grants, and loan-free financial aid packages.
