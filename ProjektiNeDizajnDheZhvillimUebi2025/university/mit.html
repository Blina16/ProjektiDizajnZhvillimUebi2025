MIT University

About MIT University

MIT University, located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, is one of the world's most prestigious institutions. Known for its rigorous academics and research excellence, Harvard offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs.

SAT Scores

The SAT score range for admitted students typically falls between 1460 and 1580, with a strong emphasis on academic excellence and extracurricular involvement.

Financial Aid

MIT operates a need-based financial aid policy, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent talented students from attending. Families earning less than $75,000 annually are not required to pay tuition.

Learn more about Harvard's financial aid policies here: Harvard Financial Aid.

Location & Campus Info

MIT University is located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. Its main campus is situated along the Charles River, just a short distance from Boston, offering students a blend of academic prestige and urban opportunities. The exact address is:

MIT University Massachusetts Hall, Cambridge, MA 02138, United States.

IELTS

Stanford generally requires a minimum IELTS score of 7.0 for undergraduate and graduate admissions. However, competitive applicants often have higher scores, especially in individual sections.

Scholarships

Coverage: Stanford financial aid can cover up to 100% of demonstrated financial need.

>Grant-Based: Most of the aid is provided as grants (which do not need to be repaid), with a smaller portion possibly as student loans or work-study programs.
